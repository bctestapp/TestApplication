<div class="SEOH1"><h1>Bellevue College Continuing Education</h1></div>
 <div>
<p>Bellevue College Continuing Education provides professional training, certificate programs and personal enrichment classes to adults in the Seattle-Bellevue area. Whether you want to enhance your professional skills, change careers or explore a new interest, we have a program for you.</p> 
 </div>

<div class="SEOH2"><h2>Receive real-world, professional training</h2></div>  
<div>
<p>Boost your earning potential today. Our instructors are experienced professionals who are currently working in their field. Classes offer you the practical, professional skills most in demand in today's workplace.</p>
 </div>
<div class="SEOH2"><h2>Earn a professional certificate in as little as 6 months</h2></div> 
<div>
<p>Bellevue College Continuing Education offers professional certificate programs in high-demand careers including Project Management, Computer Programming, Human Resources and Database Administration.
<ul>
<li>Day and evening courses</li>
<li>Start any time you like</li>
<li>Complete at your own pace</li>
</ul>
</p>
 </div>
<div class="SEOH2"><h2>Learn something new</h2></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li><strong>World Languages Institute</strong> offers classes in 18 different languages, including Spanish, French, Italian and Japanese.</li>
<li><strong>Personal enrichment programs</strong> are offered in art, fitness, finance and more.</li>
</ul>
</div>
